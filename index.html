Discover My Journey

My Journey

I was born on December 4, 1985, and my journey into Buddhism formally began in 2007 when I entered the Galduwa Forest tradition as a novice monk. In 2010, I took my higher ordination, deepening my commitment to the spiritual path.

This dedication led me to Myanmar in 2012, where I immersed myself in the study of Buddhist scriptures and meditation, further earning a Diploma in Buddha Dhamma from the International Theravada Buddhist Missionary University in 2015.

While in Myanmar, I became fluent in the local language and explored traditional Myanmar Buddhist teachings. To expand my knowledge and deepen my practice, I traveled to Thailand in 2019 to experience the revered Thai Forest Tradition at Wat Pah Nanachat Monastery.

Today, with over 16 years in the robes, my path is evolving to embrace new forms of outreach. With a deep respect for traditional Buddhist teachings, I aim to share insights through modern platforms.

My Teachings

Buddhist Meditation and Mindfulness

Grounded in years of practice across Myanmar and Thailand, my approach to meditation is rooted in both the Galduwa Forest tradition and the Thai Forest Tradition.

Pali Language Instruction

With extensive experience in teaching Pali at IIT, I offer courses designed for both beginners and advanced students, making this ancient language accessible.

Modern Wisdom Through Ancient Teachings

My teachings incorporate Buddhist philosophy into today's fast-paced world, inspiring a mindful approach to modern challenges.

Content Creation & Writing

As a Buddhist monk, my journey has taken me through various spiritual paths and cultural landscapes. Today, I am passionate about sharing these experiences through storytelling and writing. My topics range from meditation and mindfulness to emerging fields like technology and artificial intelligence.

I am especially excited about using social media as a platform to engage with a broader audience. Through thoughtful content, I aim to inspire others, explore the intersections of tradition and modernity, and offer practical insights into personal growth.
